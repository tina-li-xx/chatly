## Research Summary

| Element | Best Practice | Applied |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| Email length | 50-125 words (sweet spot: 75-100) | ✓ All emails under 100 words |
| Subject lines | 3-7 words, lowercase, specific | ✓ One subject per sequence |
| Follow-ups | Reply to same thread | ✓ Emails 2-5 are replies (no new subject) |
| Sequence length | 4-5 emails over 2-3 weeks | ✓ 5 emails per ICP |
| Timing | Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 21 | ✓ Widening gap cadence |
| Each follow-up | Adds NEW value, not "just checking in" | ✓ New angle per email |
| CTA | Single, low-friction ask | ✓ Reply-based, no URLs |
| URLs | Hurt deliverability on cold outreach | ✓ No links in emails |

---

## Tone Guide

- **Declarative identity**. state what we are, don't pitch or persuade
- **Quiet confidence**. "might be useful, might not"
- **No manufactured pain**. skip "you're losing customers" energy
- **Respect the reader**. they're smart, they can decide
- **Reply-based CTA**. "let me know if you want a link"

---

## Sequence Structure

- **Email 1 (Day 1):** Subject line + state what we are
- **Email 2 (Day 3):** Reply. specific value angle
- **Email 3 (Day 7):** Reply. different angle
- **Email 4 (Day 14):** Reply. another angle
- **Email 5 (Day 21):** Reply. breakup
